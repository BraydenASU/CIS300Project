Top 10 Games of the Past 10 Years

The top 10 games listed are based on Metacritic's Metascore ratings. All rankings support all console platforms and PC.

10. Persona 5 Royal

Everything good about the original, with even more content.

Persona 5 Royal is an enhanced version of the original Persona 5, a critically acclaimed JRPG. It features new characters, storylines, and gameplay mechanics, making it a must-play for fans of the series and newcomers alike. Along with the original Persona 5's engaging story and stylish presentation, Royal adds even more content and improvements, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade. Although it may not have the same level of critical acclaim as some of the other games on this list, it is still a fantastic game that deserves recognition for its improvements and additions to an already great game.

9. Hades II

Somehow, it's even better than the first one!

Hades II is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed roguelike game Hades. Building on the success of its predecessor, Hades II promises to deliver an even more immersive and engaging experience. With its unique blend of fast-paced action, deep storytelling, and stunning art style, Hades II is poised to be a standout title in the gaming landscape. While it may not have the same level of critical acclaim as some of the other games on this list, it is still a fantastic game that deserves recognition for its improvements and additions to an already great game. With the same addictive gameplay loop and captivating narrative, Hades II is sure to captivate both fans of the original and newcomers alike, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade.

8. Portal: Companion Collection

Two award-winning games in one collection.

The Portal: Companion Collection is a compilation of the critically acclaimed puzzle-platformer games Portal and Portal 2. Both games are renowned for their innovative gameplay mechanics, clever writing, and memorable characters. The collection offers players the opportunity to experience both games in one package, making it a must-have for fans of the series and newcomers alike. While it may not have the same level of critical acclaim as some of the other games on this list, it is still a fantastic collection that deserves recognition for bringing together two beloved games into one seamless experience. With its unique blend of humor, challenging puzzles, and engaging storytelling, the Portal: Companion Collection is sure to captivate players and solidify its place as one of the best gaming experiences of the decade.

7. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

The amazing sequel that brings new mechanics and exploration.

Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Building on the success of its predecessor, Tears of the Kingdom promises to deliver an even more immersive and engaging experience. With its unique blend of open-world exploration, innovative gameplay mechanics, and captivating storytelling, there are almost endless things you can build and explore in this game. While it may not have the same level of critical acclaim as some of the other games on this list, it is still a fantastic game that deserves recognition for its improvements and additions to an already great game. With the same addictive gameplay loop and captivating narrative, Tears of the Kingdom is sure to captivate both fans of the original, as well as new players, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade.

6. Elden Ring

The critically acclaimed open-world RPG from the creators of Dark Souls.

Elden Ring is an action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is the latest game from the creators of the critically acclaimed Dark Souls series, and it has received widespread acclaim for its open-world design, challenging gameplay, and captivating storytelling. With its unique blend of exploration, combat, and difficulty, Elden Ring offers players a rich and immersive gaming experience. While it may not have the same level of critical acclaim as some of the other games on this list, it is still a fantastic game that deserves recognition for its innovative gameplay mechanics and engaging world-building. With its addictive gameplay loop and captivating narrative, Elden Ring is sure to captivate both fans of the Dark Souls series and newcomers alike, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade.

5. The House in Fata Morgana - Dreams of the Revenants Edition

This visual novel brings a unique blend of mystery and adventure.

The House in Fata Morgana - Dreams of the Revenants Edition is a visual novel that offers a unique blend of mystery, adventure, and psychological horror. With its captivating storytelling, stunning art style, and memorable characters, it has received critical acclaim for its emotional depth and engaging narrative. While it may not have the same level of critical acclaim as some of the other games on this list, it is still a fantastic game that deserves recognition for its innovative storytelling and immersive atmosphere. With its addictive gameplay loop and captivating narrative, The House in Fata Morgana is sure to captivate both fans of visual novels and newcomers alike, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade.

4. Baldur's Gate 3

This is a fantasy RPG that allows players to create their own character and make choices that affect the story.

Baldur's Gate 3 is a fantasy RPG developed and published by Larian Studios. It is the latest installment in the Baldur's Gate series, and it has received widespread acclaim for its deep storytelling, engaging gameplay mechanics, and stunning visuals. With its unique blend of character creation, choice-driven narrative, and tactical combat, Baldur's Gate 3 offers players a rich and immersive gaming experience. While it may not have the same level of critical acclaim as some of the other games on this list, it is still a fantastic game that deserves recognition for its innovative gameplay mechanics and engaging world-building. With its addictive gameplay loop and captivating narrative, Baldur's Gate 3 is sure to captivate both fans of the series and newcomers alike, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade.

3. Super Mario Odyssey

A huge open-world platformer with incredible creativity

Super Mario Odyssey is a 3D platformer game that features a huge open world for players to explore. It offers incredible creativity in its level design and gameplay mechanics, making it a standout title in the Mario franchise. Using your hat, Cappy, you can capture and control various objects and enemies, adding a unique twist to the traditional Mario gameplay. With its vibrant visuals, catchy music, and engaging gameplay, Super Mario Odyssey has received critical acclaim and is considered one of the best games of the decade. Although it isn't number one on the list, it is still a fantastic game that deserves recognition for its innovative gameplay mechanics. Super Mario Odyssey is sure to captivate both fans of the series and newcomers alike, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade.

2. Red Dead Redemption 2

A story-driven Western adventure that will make you screem "YEE-HAW!"

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world action-adventure game developed and published by Rockstar Games. Set in the late 1800s, it follows the story of Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang, as he navigates the challenges of life in the American frontier. With its stunning visuals, immersive world, and compelling narrative, Red Dead Redemption 2 has received widespread acclaim and is considered one of the best games of the decade. With one of the best stories in gaming, and a huge open world to explore, Red Dead Redemption 2 is sure to captivate anyone that picks it up, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade.

1. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Proof they did it right the first time

Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an open-world action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. It follows the story of Link, who awakens from a long slumber to find the kingdom of Hyrule in ruins. With its stunning visuals, immersive world, and innovative gameplay mechanics, Breath of the Wild has received widespread acclaim and is considered one of the best games of the decade. With its unique blend of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat, Breath of the Wild offers players a rich and immersive gaming experience. It is sure to captivate both fans of the series and newcomers alike, solidifying its place as one of the best games of the decade. There is a reason this game made it to number one on the list, and that is because it is a masterpiece of game design that has set a new standard for open-world games. With its addictive gameplay loop and captivating narrative, Breath of the Wild is a must-play for anyone looking for an unforgettable gaming experience.
